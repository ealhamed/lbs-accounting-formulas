Direct vs Indirect Cash Flow

Module 4 — Interactive Explainer

The ELI5 Analogy

Both methods answer the same question: "How much actual cash did operations generate?" They always produce the same number — just different routes to get there.

Direct Method Check every receipt in your wallet. Add up what came in, subtract what went out. Done.

"Show me the cash."
Indirect Method Start with your reported profit. Then undo every accounting trick that wasn't real cash.

"Start with profit, undo the non-cash stuff."
