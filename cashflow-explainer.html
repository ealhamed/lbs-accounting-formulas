Cash Collected from Customers

Revenue isn't cash. This formula bridges the gap.

The Formula
Cash Collected = Revenue + ΔDeferred Revenue − ΔReceivables
Receivables up = earned but not collected (subtract). Deferred Revenue up = collected but not earned (add).
+ customers owe more   − old debts collected
+ cash collected upfront   − prepayments delivered

Indirect Method

Starts with profit, undoes non-cash items

+ Δ Deferred Rev +$30
Cash from Ops+$150
Direct = +$150  |  Indirect = +$150 — Always the same.
Direct = "show me the actual cash flows"
Indirect = "start with profit, adjust for timing differences"
Same Cash from Ops. Different route. The formula above is how the Indirect method derives the customer cash line.
